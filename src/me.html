CryptoSummer by NOVA

The CryptoSummer was a summer project about cryptocurrencies and blockchain. I had my first real exposure to blockchain technology and the ecosystem which flourished with the birth of the first cryptocurrency. Our project was aimed towards the solid waste menagement, basically the idea is to reward people for separating and recycling solid urban waste and the reward would come in the form of a digital currency.
